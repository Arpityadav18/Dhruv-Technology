Training and Workshops

We are a company which provides Land Survey and civil equipment training in India, land surveying is a process that is used to determine the features and boundaries of a property and to determine easements, and encroachments, to find out how to develop or build on the land, as well as to maintain local codes and regulations.

  • Land Surveyors measure distances, angles, and positions to develop a map of the field.
  • The objective of this Training is to provide basic knowledge of digital surveying and mapping of the earth's surface using Total Station, DGPS, Auto-Level, and mapping software. The Training starts with an introduction to land surveying followed by fundamentals of surveying instruments and their working & measurements for land surveying and mapping fundamentals, digital surveying techniques, data reduction, etc. After completion of the Course, we will provide certification, and also students will be able to demonstrate standard techniques to locate and determine three-dimensional coordinates of points on the ground using surveying instruments.
